Our Mission

"To engineer high-yield, premium stoneground grain products through sustainable agricultural pipelines, feeding communities and reinforcing industrial B2B food supplies with absolute safety and transparency."

Our History

Founded over 15 years ago, Siirwa Millers began as a localized community mill specialized in traditional whole-meal processing. Recognizing a deep regional demand for high-protein baking flours and climate-resilient staple grains like pearl millet, we scaled our facility infrastructure to support automated extraction loops.

Today, our operations manage a 15,000 MT annual milling processing capacity, serving industrial networks, large-scale bakeries, and institutional distribution hubs without sacrificing our foundational ties to local farming cooperatives.
